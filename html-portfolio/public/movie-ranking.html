The most interesting movies I watched in 2024

(in no particular order!)

Lost in Translation (2003)

I love those movies where you need to pay attention, because there's not a lot of loud music.. The dialogue here is great, the vibes are just right.

Ordinary People (1980)

Powerful movie. Stellar acting. From the times where a movie had to be THAT good to get an Oscar.

Dead Ringers (1988)

Another classic. The soundtrack is misleading in the best possible way. A haunting movie that will stick with you.
